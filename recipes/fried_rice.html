Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of rice, cooked without salt
  • Chicken breast
  • 1 Chicken stock cube
  • 2 med size onions
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Spring onions
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 3 eggs
  • Maggi cube
  • Soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil


Directions

  1. Heat your deep frying pan or wok pan.
  2. Add 2tbsp of olive oil.
  3. Add the chopped onions.
  4. Add the chicken breast marinated in soy sauce and stir. Allow it to cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the carrots and stir and leave it on for about 2 minutes.
  6. Add the bell peppers.
  7. Make a hole in the middle of your pan and add your eggs and make scrambled eggs out of it. (You can do this separately in a different pan and add it.
  8. If you did not cook your rice with chicken stock then you can now add in your stock cube.
  9. Add 2 tbsp of curry powder.
  10. Stir all the mixture together and leave it on for about 3-5 minutes.
  11. Add your cooked rice and give it a very good stir.
  12. Add the spring onions and stir.
  13. Add some soy sauce according to your taste and give it a good stir.
  14. Serve your fried rice with some salad or chicken and enjoy.
